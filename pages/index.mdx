# Introduction

**Flash Promos** is a [Shopify](https://www.shopify.com/) app built to let merchants create, manage and schedule promotions, discounts and price changes in a really simple way, but also **blazing fast**.

    * **Fast**: Flash Promos is blazing fast and doesn't require any setup.
    * **Simple**: Flash Promos is simple and easy to use.
    * **Frugal**: Flash Promos is frugal and has a a small set of features that let you manage prices without headaches.
    * **Free Tier**: Flash Promos has free tier to get you started.
